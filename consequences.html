Positive Impacts We Aim For

  • More mental clarity though taking time to reflect.
  • Greater creativity from avoiding rushed decisions
  • Less stress, resulting in better well-being and work-life balance.
  • More thoughtful, meaningful contributions to society and personal projects.

By embracing delays and thoughtful procrastinations, our movement brings about these consequences,
creating a culture of better productivity and mental health.
