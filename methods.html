Our Methods

Our approach to procrastination is carefully crafted over years of... waiting. Here are our top methods:

  • The 5-Minute Delay: Just wait 5 more minutes before starting. You'll be amazed at how much can happen in those 5 minutes... or not.
  • The research Loop: Spend your time reasearching endless of starting the task. Learning is important, right?
  • The Power Nap: A well-timed nap can delay any project and recharged your energy... for more procrastination.

Our methods help you to stay calm, cool, and collected while everything else waits. Why rush when the clock is still ticking?
