Why Join Us?

Have you ever started a project early, only to find out you did more work than necessary because things changed?
That's why procrastiination is actually a smart move. Here's why:

  • Better ideas come later: Waiting until the last minute can spark creativity under pressure.
  • Less effort, more result : By delaying, you might find that the problem solves itself or becomes irrelevant!
  • Stress-free living: No need to worry about long-term plans when you focus on enjoying today.

Proscrastination is about take control of your own time and deceding when you're ready to act.
