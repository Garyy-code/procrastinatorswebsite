Who are We

The "Procrastinators Movement" was founded in 2024 by group of like-minded
individuals who believe in the philosophy of taking time before acting.
Our mission is to encourage people to pause, promoting mental health, and creativity.

Our Association

The "International Procrastinators Society" (IPS) supports our movement and
provides ressources for those who embrace thoughtful delays.
Our association aims to bring together procrastinators from
around the world, offering tools for productivity though deliberate inaction.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where pauses are embraced as key part of success.
Whether you're taking your time to thinks of new ideas, or reflecting on your
goals, the Procrastinators Movement is here to support you.
